Our Mission

We believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive in a world filled with love, happiness, and opportunity. Our mission is to be the guiding gardener for children who have lost their parents, nurturing them with care, support, and resources to ensure they blossom into resilient, confident, and compassionate individuals.

To create a world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to grow up in a nurturing environment that enables them to reach their full potential. We envision a society where love, care, and opportunities are abundant for every child, ensuring they become positive contributors to the global community.

We are dedicated to being the guardian, mentor, and advocate for every child, ensuring they not only survive but thrive in a world full of possibilities.

WE BELIEVE IN SERVING HUMANITY REGARDLESS OF CLASS, CREED AND RELIGION

We believe that all children are loved by God. Throughout Scripture, we see God’s heart for the fatherless and James 1:27 makes very clear the mandate to care for orphaned children to His glory. We believe that one of the responsibilities of God’s people is to care for widows, orphans, and those who have been marginalized by the economic and social systems of the world.

It is to that end that we come alongside the body of Christ to support orphanages and children’s homes.
